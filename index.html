TOP 1

Padar (Komodo Island)

A visit to Labuan Bajo wouldn't be complete if you haven't been to Komodo Island. This is where animals in the form of ancient lizards live and roam. Recognized as a world heritage site by UNESCO and one of the seven wonders of the natural world from the New 7 Wonders of Nature, Komodo Island is a must-see spot in Labuan Bajo. The exoticism of the endemic animal Komodo is increasingly enriched by its enchanting natural landscape. The natural beauty of this island stretches out from the lush trees, green hills and charming beaches. It'll make you forget to go home!

TOP 2

Kelingking Beach

Located on Nusa Penida Island, Kelingking Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches and a favorite photo spot for tourists when visiting the island. The entrance to this beach is on a cliff with a hill that extends and juts out into the sea. If you want to enjoy the beauty of Kelingking Beach, tourists have to struggle a little. They will climb down a steep cliff with a slope of around 70-80 degrees and hold on to a wooden fence. However, they can also use a boat to get to this beach. One of the activities you can do at Kelingking Beach apart from enjoying the view is diving and observing dozens of manta rays at Manta Point.

TOP 3

Wayag Island

A tourist attraction in Raja Ampat that you shouldn't miss is Wayag. Wayag is an island consisting of a group of small islands filled with trees. Wayag Island is one of the icons in Raja Ampat and is a favorite of many tourists. You can take photos with a beautiful group of small islands in the background.

TOP 4

Gili Trawangan

Gili Trawangan is the third largest island to the northwest of Lombok. This island is famous for its enchanting underwater natural beauty. Even though it is not as busy as Carita beach in Anyer, Banten, Gili Trawangan still offers tranquility that is hard to find in other tourist attractions. On Gili Trawangan, tourists can do various exciting activities such as snorkeling, diving, sunbathing, swimming, sitting relaxing on the beach, and hunting for cool photos to add to their album gallery on social media. If you want to stay overnight, there are many accommodations such as villas, hotels and resorts around the beach area.
